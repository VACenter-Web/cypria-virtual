About Us

Founded in the not-so-distant past (in July 2022), Air Transat Virtual was born with the simple ambition of giving the beauty of continental Canada the traffic it deserves in Infinite Flight! We boast flights to over 60 destinations, even as far as gleaming Greece and the dazzling Dominican Republic, we undoubtedly have something to offer every pilot!

We are here to provide you with the love and care through virtual aviation like no other else. We are Air Transat Virtual, a dedicated VA that is ready to enhance your experience in the world of Infinite Flight. So join us to see what we provide you with...
